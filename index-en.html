World Wide Web

The service was created in 1980 by Tim Berners-Lee and means "global reach network". It is a system of hypermedia documents that are interconnected and executed on the Internet and can be in the form of videos, sounds, hypertexts and figures. It acts as an information integrator, so that the vast majority of information available on the Internet can be accessed in a simple and consistent way on different platforms. It’s in general terms the graphical interface of the Internet.

HyperText Markup Language

Language invented by Tim, in 1990, is what makes it possible to present information on the Internet. The initial purpose was to make it possible to access and exchange information between scientists from different universities and to solve the problem of communication and dissemination of research between him and his group. Its solution, combined with the then emerging public Internet, gained worldwide attention. What you see when you open a web page is your browser’s interpretation of HTML.
